# Release Notes

## Version 1.19
- **Speed Round** — new 60-second timed study mode: answer multiple choice questions as fast as you can, track your best score per deck
- **Per-card accuracy tracking** — see how well you know each card with a correct/incorrect breakdown in the card editor
- **Incorrect cards repeat in session** — cards you miss are shown again later in the same study session for immediate reinforcement
- **Difficulty badges** — Hard and Tricky badges highlight struggling cards right in your deck
- **Sort by Difficulty** — sort your deck by accuracy to focus on the cards that need the most work
- App renamed to MemoryMutt with a fresh icon

## Version 1.18
- Daily card limit setting to control how many cards you study per session
- Haptic feedback added across all study modes
- Miscellaneous bug fixes and stability improvements

## Version 1.17
- Tip Jar — support the app with an optional in-app tip
- Privacy policy added within the app

## Version 1.16
- Hangman study mode
- Match game study mode
- Multiple choice study mode
- Starter decks: World Capitals, US States, US Presidents, Spanish, French

## Version 1.15
- Wikipedia links on cards for quick reference
- Reverse card mode — study back-to-front

## Version 1.0
- Initial release
- Flashcard creation and deck management
- Spaced repetition with SM-2 algorithm
- Progress tracking and statistics
